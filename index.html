A bit more about me

My learning career

I recently graduated from the University of Southampton with a BSc in Economics where I earned an Upper Second Class (2:1) degree. During my studies I led an Econometrics project which sharpened my decision-making and people management skills, and I achieved an 86% grade. I also excelled in Portfolio Theory and Financial Markets with a score of 83%.

My academic journey was complemented by hands-on experience running an online retail arbitrage business where I identified market opportunities, maintained 5 star customer service, and developed strong organisational and time management skills. Additionally I participated in job simulations with JPMorgan and HSBC where I analysed financial data and built financial models, further honing my analytical abilities.

Outside of academics, I captained my school rugby team and was recognized as the coaches player of the year in university football. My commitment to fitness by working out six days a week has taught me the importance of consistency and resilience in achieving long-term goals.

I am now eager to bring my strong academic background, practical experience, and leadership skills to my career.
